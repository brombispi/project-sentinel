The DigiRettung Experience

Our Goal

People will not remember every technical detail of the recovery.

They will remember how they felt during one of the most stressful moments involving their digital life.

Our responsibility is not only to recover data.

Our responsibility is to reduce fear, build trust and give people hope through a structured, transparent and professional process.

Every customer should leave thinking:

“I felt someone genuinely cared about recovering what mattered to me.”

The DigiRettung Principles

1. Listen before diagnosing.
2. Protect the original before attempting recovery.
3. Explain every important step in simple language.
4. Never promise what cannot be guaranteed.
5. Document everything.
6. Respect every customer’s memories as if they were our own.
7. Deliver recovered data in an organised and understandable way.
8. Continue improving every recovery through experience.

DRS-1 exists to support these principles.

Technology is not the goal.

Trust is.
